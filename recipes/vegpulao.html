Veg Pulao Recipes

Description:

Veg Pulao is a fragrant rice dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, and aromatic spices. It's a one-pot meal that's easy to prepare and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1-2 cardamom pods
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  3. In a pot, heat ghee or oil over medium heat.
  4. Add cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom, and bay leaf. Sauté for a minute.
  5. Add sliced onions and green chilies. Cook until onions are golden brown.
  6. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  7. Add mixed vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add the soaked rice and stir gently to mix with the vegetables.
  9. Add water and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  10. Cover and cook until the rice is fluffy and all the water is absorbed.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  12. Serve hot with raita or salad.
  13. Enjoy your delicious Veg Pulao!
